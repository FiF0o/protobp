Architecture & Design Assurance


As part of the proposed FMO (Future Mode of Operations) for Upstream by Wipro, several key transformational activities are planned to be delivered in 2016 and beyond. Underpinning these transformational activities, a Wipro AWG (Architecture Working Group) is established with the following objectives.


  • Establish a formal communication mechanism between Wipro architects working for Upstream Programme across all areas (i.e. transformation, transition & service delivery) with regular interactions.

  • Ensure that changes are coordinated across various initiatives from architecture perspective and minimal / no impact to the service delivery / operations.

  • Perform peer reviews and Design Assurance of the architecture and design deliverables pertaining to the transformational initiatives.

  • Ensure the ACP compliance (Architecture Common Process) for all transformational projects / Programmes.

  • Ensure that knowledge is shared across the architect community.

  • Capture, track and review the Architecture KPIs / metrics and present to BP stakeholders
